Our Story

KG1 Studio was envisioned as a sanctuary for movement, arts, and creative exploration — a space where artists, teachers, and learners alike can discover, refine, and express their craft.

Founded by legendary Bharatanatyam dancer Priyadarsini Govind, the studio began as her private practice space, a sanctuary where she honed her craft and nurtured countless choreographies. Today, KG1 opens its doors to the world, inviting a new generation of artistes & creators to create, learn, and share within its walls.

At its core, KG1 Studio celebrates creativity as a shared language. Located in central Chennai, KG1 Studio offers a refined black-box environment tailored for versatility and focus. From rehearsals and workshops to live performances and shoots, every element of the space — lighting, acoustics, and layout — is designed to adapt seamlessly to your creative needs.

Capable of integrating with all AV equipment, and featuring adaptable seating for intimate audiences within a professional yet inviting ambience, KG1 Studio has become a preferred destination for masterclasses, wellness sessions, and boutique cultural events. Its reputation is backed by a 5/5 community rating, and its doors are open to creators looking to teach, perform, or produce high-impact content in a well-maintained, centrally located space.

Creative Collaborations

A space for artists to teach, perform, and grow — together.

KG1 Studio opens its doors to artists and trainers seeking a space to share their art, teach their craft, and curate meaningful cultural experiences.

Located in the heart of Chennai, KG1 Studio is a 1,000 sq. ft. black-box space designed for versatility and focus. Featuring blackout drapes, flexible lighting, and compatibility with all AV setups, it offers an ideal setting for workshops, masterclasses, rehearsals, and small performances. The studio comfortably accommodates up to 50 participants and serves as a blank canvas that adapts seamlessly to every creative form.
